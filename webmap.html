    Identify features

    • Click a block or parcel polygon to open a summary card at the click location.
    • The card lists codes, area, geometry status, cultivation status, harvest fields, and notes when present.
    • Use × on the card or press Escape to close it.
    • Lat / lon grid (REFERENCE in the layer list) is sparse by default; toggle it off there if you need a cleaner view.
    • Place next to Coords opens a place-name search (OpenStreetMap) to fly the map like a quick “Google Earth” lookup.
